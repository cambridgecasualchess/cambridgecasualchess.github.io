At Cambridge Casual Chess club, we aim to provide a place for people to play over the board chess in a social/casual setting. We welcome all levels, and you'll typically find players with ratings ranging from 600 upto around 2000. So whether you're a beginner or a seasoned player you're guarenteed to find a good game.

You'll find us every Tuesday from 7:00 PM to 10:30 PM at the Grain and Hop Store on Reagent Street.

We hope to see you there for an evening of friendly competition and camraderie.
